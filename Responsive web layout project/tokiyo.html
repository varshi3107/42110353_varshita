TOKIYO

Tokyo is the capital of Japan and one of the most populous cities in the world, with a population of over 14 million residents within the city proper as of 2023.

Tokyo serves as Japan's economic center and the seat of both the Japanese government and the Emperor of Japan. The Tokyo Metropolitan Government administers Tokyo's central 23 special wards, various commuter towns and suburbs in its western area, and two outlying island chains known as the Tokyo Islands.
